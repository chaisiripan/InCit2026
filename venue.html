Venue

The 10th International Conference on Information Technology (InCIT 2026) will be proudly hosted at Siam University, one of Thailand's leading private higher education institutions located in the heart of Bangkok's bustling Phasi Charoen district.

Welcome to Siam University

Bangkok, Thailand

About Siam University

Siam University is a premier private higher education institution located in Bangkok, Thailand. Established in 1965, it has grown to become one of the country's leading universities, offering a wide range of academic programs. The university is deeply committed to academic excellence, innovation, and preparing students to meet global challenges. With modern facilities and a vibrant international community, Siam University provides an inspiring environment for both learning and research.

Faculty of Information Technology

The Faculty of Information Technology at Siam University is dedicated to developing world-class IT professionals. The IT faculty emphasizes practical skills, cutting-edge technology research, and industry collaboration. By integrating theoretical knowledge with hands-on experience in areas such as software engineering, data science, and cybersecurity, the faculty continuously drives technological advancement and serves as a vital hub for the IT community in Thailand.
