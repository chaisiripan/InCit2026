Hotels

Hotels

For the convenience of our attendees, we have prepared a list of recommended hotels located near Siam University or along the BTS Skytrain / MRT Subway routes.

The Quarter Wutthakat by UHG

A modern and comfortable hotel located just one BTS stop away from Bang Wa Station. Ideal for attendees looking for convenience and a great price-to-quality ratio.

  • BTS Wutthakat Station
  • 10 mins to Siam University
  • ~ 1,200 - 1,500 THB/night
Visit Website

Kaya Heritage Hotel

A boutique heritage hotel offering a unique Thai cultural experience. It is located relatively close to the university and offers a quiet, relaxing atmosphere.

  • Pinklao / Bangkok Noi Area
  • 15 mins to Siam University
  • ~ 1,400 - 1,800 THB/night
Visit Website

Avani+ Riverside Bangkok

For attendees seeking luxury, this riverside hotel offers stunning views of the Chao Phraya River, upscale dining, and premium amenities with direct mall access.

  • Charoen Nakhon Road
  • 25 mins to Siam University
  • ~ 4,000 - 6,000 THB/night
Visit Website

Ibis Bangkok Riverside

A comfortable, value-for-money hotel situated right on the banks of the Chao Phraya River, offering a refreshing outdoor pool and easy ferry access across the river.

  • Charoen Nakhon 17
  • Krung Thon Buri BTS
  • ~ 1,500 - 2,200 THB/night
Visit Website

Siam Champs Elyseesi

Elegant boutique hotel blending classic European architecture with modern Thai touches, located near Bangkok's historic Old Town and major cultural attractions.

  • Ratchadamnoen Klang
  • 20 mins to Siam University
  • ~ 2,000 - 3,500 THB/night
Visit Website

Booking Disclaimer

The conference organizers do not handle hotel reservations. Please contact the hotels directly or use your preferred online booking platform (Agoda, Booking.com, Expedia) to secure your room. We recommend booking early, especially during the high tourist season in November.
