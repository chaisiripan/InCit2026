Transportation

Transportation to Siam University

Siam University is conveniently located in the Phasi Charoen district of Bangkok and is highly accessible via public transportation networks, particularly the BTS Skytrain and MRT Subway systems.

MRT (Blue Line)

The most convenient way to reach the university is via the MRT Blue Line. You can get off at Bang Wa Station (Interchange) or Phetkasem 48 Station.

BTS Skytrain (Silom Line)

Take the BTS Silom Line directly to the terminal station, Bang Wa Station (S12). From there, it is a short walking distance (approx. 10-15 mins) or a quick taxi/motorcycle ride to the campus.

Taxi & Ride-Hailing

Simply instruct your driver or pin your location on Grab/Bolt to "Siam University" (มหาวิทยาลัยสยาม) located on Phetkasem Road.

From the Airports

Suvarnabhumi Airport (BKK)

  • Airport Rail Link (ARL) + BTS: Take the ARL from the airport to Phaya Thai Station. Transfer to the BTS Sukhumvit Line, ride one stop to Siam Station, and transfer to the BTS Silom Line heading to Bang Wa Station (S12). (Approx. 1.5 hours)
  • Public Taxi / Grab: Proceed to the official taxi stand on Level 1. The trip takes about 1 hour depending on traffic. (Highway tolls apply).

Don Mueang Airport (DMK)

  • SRT Red Line + MRT: Take the SRT Red Line from the airport to Bang Sue Grand Station. Transfer to the MRT Blue Line and get off at Bang Wa Station or Phetkasem 48 Station. (Approx. 1.5 hours)
  • Public Taxi / Grab: Follow signs to the official taxi queue. The journey typically takes about 45 mins to 1 hour depending on traffic. (Highway tolls apply).
